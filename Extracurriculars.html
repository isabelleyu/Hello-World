TEDxDesignTechHighSchool

TEDxDesignTechHighSchool is a student run event that promotes "ideas worth spreading". Our goal for hosting TEDxDesignTechHighSchool is to encourage students to speak towards a different audience and to get more comfortable with public speaking. My schools primary focus is to use the design thinking process to create of unique ideas or concepts, therefore this event will help students get their ideas spread to a different medium.
